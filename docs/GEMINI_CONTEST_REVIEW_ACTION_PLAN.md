# GEMINI Contest Review Action Plan

## 1) Executive summary
Paradiso should prioritize contest participation where its current strengths in immigration law/manual data interpretation, structured reference guidance, and public-service framing are most aligned with judging criteria. Based on the Gemini review, Paradiso should immediately focus on the law-data contest track, apply stronger legal-safety positioning, and narrow the demo scope for execution speed and risk control.

## 2) Competition priority decision
- **제2회 법령데이터 활용 아이디어 공모전**: **GO / primary focus**
- **2026 국민공공외교사업**: **Conditional-Go / defer unless reframed as a campaign**
- **제4회 문화체육관광 인공지능·데이터 활용 공모전**: **No-Go / deprioritize**

## 3) Key risks identified
- AI hallucination risk
- legal authority overclaim risk
- possible confusion with legal consultation, administrative agency work, or official immigration decisions
- lack of visible source/citation grounding

## 4) Required positioning change
- from "visa/residence problem-solving platform"
- to "public legal and immigration manual information access assistant"

## 5) Required language rules
- avoid claiming to solve, decide, approve, diagnose, or handle visa procedures
- use wording such as reference information, public legal/manual sources, source-grounded guidance, and final confirmation by immigration authorities or qualified professionals

## 6) Required disclaimer text
Paradiso is a reference information tool based on public immigration laws, visa manuals, and residence manuals. It does not provide legally binding advice, administrative representation, application filing, eligibility determination, or official immigration decisions. Users must confirm final requirements with the immigration office, Hi Korea, or qualified professionals.

## 7) Two-week action plan
- add disclaimer
- strengthen source/citation presentation
- focus demo on one scenario
- prepare law-data competition narrative
- avoid automatic application generation
- avoid collecting passport numbers, alien registration numbers, or other sensitive personal data

## 8) Recommended demo scenario
- D-2 international student exploring D-10 job-seeking status after graduation

## 9) Deferred or cut items
- Culture Track for MCST contest
- automatic application generation
- personal-data-based eligibility diagnosis
- broad UI redesign
- large-scale visa_data.json normalization
